Welcome to Noor Islamic App (com.noorislamic.app). Your privacy is sacred to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our app and services. By using Noor Islamic App, you agree to the practices described here. If you disagree, please discontinue use.

We are committed to upholding the highest standards of Islamic ethics and transparency. Your data is handled with integrity, never sold, and used solely to enrich your spiritual journey.

manage_search Information We Collect

📌 Automatically Collected Data

We do not require registration or store personally identifiable information. However, for core functionality (prayer times, Qibla, Hadith of the Day), the app may access:

  • Location Data – Only when you grant permission, to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. This data remains on your device and is never uploaded.
  • Device Information – Minimal technical logs (crash reports, performance) for improving the app experience.
  • Local Storage – We store app preferences (language, calculation method, bookmarks, favorites) locally using secure device storage. No personal identifiers are saved externally.

📌 Information You Provide

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us (e.g., via email for feedback). In that case, we only use your contact details to respond and then delete them.

data_usage How We Use Information

  • To provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction based on your location.
  • To customize app experience (language, translation preferences).
  • To improve the app by analyzing anonymized crash reports.
  • To respond to your feedback or support requests.

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. All data usage aligns with the app's spiritual purpose only.

share Data Sharing & Third‑Party Services

We share minimal data only when necessary for app functionality:

  • Location Services – Handled by your device OS; we do not store or transmit location externally.
  • Notification Services – Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) may be used to deliver prayer time alerts. No personal data is linked to these notifications.
  • Analytics – We may use anonymous, aggregated analytics (e.g., Google Analytics for Firebase) to understand app performance. No personal identifiers are included.

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data according to applicable laws.

shield_lock Data Security

We implement strong security measures including encryption for data in transit (HTTPS) and secure local storage. However, no method is 100% secure. We strive to protect your information with the best available technology, and we advise you to keep your device safe.

gavel Your Rights

  • You can disable location access at any time via device settings.
  • You may clear app data to remove all locally stored preferences and bookmarks.
  • You can opt out of notifications through the app settings or device settings.
  • If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal data, please contact us to request deletion.

cookie Cookies & WebView

Our app may display web content (e.g., privacy policy) in a WebView. We do not use persistent cookies; any temporary session data is cleared upon closing the app. Third‑party websites linked in the app are not governed by this policy.

update Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
